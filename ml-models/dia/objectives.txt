A. Expressive Intensity Indicators (from anxiety / depression theory) - Saturation Based
    Measured by:
    - Line pressure
    - Stroke density
    - Shading intensity
    - Darkness distribution
    Output example:
    “High stroke pressure and dense shading detected”

B. Spatial & Scale Indicators (from depression, withdrawal, self-esteem theory)
    Measured by:
    - Figure-to-canvas ratio
    - Page usage %
    - Center vs corner placement
    - Confinement
    Output example:
    “Primary figure occupies 18% of canvas area”

C. Structural Completeness Indicators (from self & development theory)
    Measured by:
    - Missing body parts
    - Head–body proportion
    - Limb detection confidence
    - Symmetry
    Output example:
    “Hands not detected in human figure”

D. Relational Representation Indicators (optional if family drawings exist)
    Measured by:
    - Distance between figures
    - Relative size
    - Grouping vs isolation
    - Presence/absence
    Output example:
    “Self-figure positioned separately from others”
